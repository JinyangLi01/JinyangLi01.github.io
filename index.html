About me

I am a third-year Ph.D. student in the Computer Science and Engineering Department at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. H. V. Jagadish. I am a member of the Database group.

My current research focuses on data ethics: fairness, diversity and bias issues in data management systems.

I got my bachelor's degree from Peking University.
